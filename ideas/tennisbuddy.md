# TennisBuddy

Finding tennis partners with a similar skill level and compatible schedule can be difficult. Many players rely on local Facebook groups, word of mouth, or random encounters at public courts.

TennisBuddy would help players create profiles, share their experience level, availability, and preferred playing locations, then connect with other players nearby. Users could schedule matches, maintain a list of regular partners, and leave ratings or reviews after playing together.

The goal is to make it easier for recreational players to build a reliable network of hitting partners and spend more time playing tennis.

## Potential Data Sources

- Location and mapping APIs
- User-generated player profiles
- Court and facility information
