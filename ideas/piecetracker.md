# PieceTracker

Musicians often manage practice notes, repertoire, performance history, and learning goals across notebooks, spreadsheets, and multiple apps. As a repertoire grows, it becomes increasingly difficult to organize and track progress.

PieceTracker would provide a centralized platform where musicians can organize pieces they're learning, log practice sessions, track performance readiness, and maintain a history of performances. Users could categorize pieces by status, difficulty, composer, and instrument.

The goal is to help musicians stay organized, monitor their progress, and maintain a complete record of their musical development over time.

## Potential Data Sources

- MusicBrainz API
- Spotify API
- User-generated practice and repertoire data
